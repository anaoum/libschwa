On Saturday evening , Her Excellency the Governor , as Patron of the Gay & Lesbian Counselling Service of NSW , accompanied by Sir Nicholas Shehadie , officially opened joint premises with Twenty Ten Gay & Lesbian Youth Support , at Newtown .

Later , Her Excellency , as Patron , accompanied by Sir Nicholas , presented prizes at the 2004 Sydney International Piano Competition of Australia Finalists Recital , in the Concert Hall , Sydney Opera House .

Yesterday , Her Excellency , as Patron , was Guest of Honour at The Wayside Chapel 's Annual Classical Music Fundraising Concert , at the Parish Church of St James , King Street , Sydney
